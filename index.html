About me

I am an Industrial Designer, that seeks to learn from various disciplines outside my career, which has motivated me to participate in multidisciplinary projects of various kinds. I look forward to transcending in design through change generating projects with a human centered approach. As part of my professional experience I have worked as an Industrial and UX/UI design intern at Mabe, as a Jr. Designer in Rehrig Pacific Company and right now as part of my professional training regarding user experiences and interfaces I am part the Next-Gen program at Ksquare University as a UX / UI Associate.
